🧸 Retail Sales & Inventory Analytics Dashboard

Power BI Project | Toy Store Business Intelligence Solution

This project delivers an interactive Power BI dashboard for analyzing retail sales, inventory performance, product trends, and store-level insights for toy stores across Mexico. It transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights to improve revenue, profitability, and inventory management.

📌 Project Overview

Duration: Jan 2022 – Sep 2023 (638 days)

Stores: 50 stores across 29 cities

Products: 35 unique items

Transactions: 829K+ sales records

🔍 Business Challenges

No centralized analytics system

Inventory imbalances (stockouts & overstock)

Lost revenue due to poor inventory planning

Decision-making based on assumptions instead of data

🎯 Project Objectives

Monitor overall sales and profitability performance

Identify top products and categories

Analyze time-based sales trends

Detect inventory risks and stock issues

Evaluate store and city-level performance

📈 Dashboard Insights
💰 Revenue Insights

Toys contribute 35% of total sales

Revenue peaks in December (holiday season)

Sales dip in July–August (summer slowdown)

💵 Profit Insights

Toys & Electronics generate the highest profit

Profit margin declined from 32% → 27%

Downtown stores generate highest profit

📦 Product Insights

Lego Bricks: Highest revenue ($2.4M)

Jenga: High margin (70%) but low sales

Dino Egg: Lowest margin → needs pricing strategy

Art & Crafts: High volume but low order value

📊 Inventory Insights

77 products out of stock → immediate restocking needed

Electronics has only 12 days of supply

15K products in low stock

Games hold excess inventory but low sales

🏪 Store Insights

Mexico City: Top-performing location

Downtown stores generate highest revenue & profit

Airport stores underperform significantly

Cities like Guadalajara & Monterrey show expansion potential

📅 Sales Insights

Friday & Saturday: Highest sales days

Monday: Lowest sales → opportunity for promotions

Sales growth fluctuates significantly → needs stabilization

💡 Business Recommendations

Focus on high-performing categories (Toys, Electronics)

Restock out-of-stock and low-stock items immediately

Optimize pricing for low-margin products

Run promotions during low-demand months (July–August)

Expand in high-performing cities and Downtown locations

Reduce inventory for slow-moving categories

⚠️ Project Limitations

Limited historical data (638 days only)

Static dataset (no real-time updates)

No customer-level data available

Simplified inventory analysis (no safety stock or lead time modeling)

🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how Power BI dashboards can transform raw retail data into actionable insights, enabling businesses to optimize sales, inventory, and profitability through data-driven decision-making.
